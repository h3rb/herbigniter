A Few Tips
  1. To create a quick defect report in Excel, just click Export Data (above), then click Download.

  2. On the New and Edit screens, send an email notification to other team members by clicking the envelope.

  3. To see an audit trail of all the changes that have been made to an issue, click on the ID, then History.

  4. Print pages in landscape mode.

  5. If printing from Internet Explorer, enable prettier reports: Tools/Internet Options/Advanced/Print Background colors.

  6. The Help button above is context-sensitive -- the text changes depending on which screen you're on.
